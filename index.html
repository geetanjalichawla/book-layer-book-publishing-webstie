Welcome

book layer is home to some of the most well-known names in fiction and non-fiction, poetry and prose, across different genres. And we are always on the lookout for talent and stories.

If you have an exceptional idea for a book, perhaps some chapters or even a summary, and the gumption to put yourself out there for the world to read, you have come to the right place.

Our publishing house has specialized divisions that focus on discovering and publishing different kinds of voices for a wide range of readers.

Ebury Publishing and Vintage Publishing and layers Press are English-language publishers for adults.

Hind Pocket Books publishes in Hindi.

If you’re ready to publish a book, but don’t know what to do after you have your book finished… Going with a publishing house might make sense. But then, it also might not.

You wrote a book? Congrats! … Now what?

You’ve got two basic options for publishing your book: traditional publishing and self-publishing.

But how do you decide which is best for you? Depending on your personal goals, each option could be your saving grace or your biggest downfall.

Choosing the best publishing option will set you up for success. But this means understanding them. For the purpose of this post, we’re going to explore publishing houses and if this method is right for you.
